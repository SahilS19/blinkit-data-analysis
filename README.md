## Overview
This project analyzes sales data from **Blinkit ‚Äì India‚Äôs Last Minute Delivery App** using Power BI.  
The goal is to convert raw sales data into meaningful insights around outlet performance, product trends, and customer behavior.

The dashboard is designed to be simple, interactive, and business-focused.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze overall sales and revenue trends  
- Compare outlet performance by **location tier, size, and type**  
- Identify top-selling product categories  
- Understand customer ratings and item visibility  
- Support data-driven business decisions  

---

## Key Metrics
- Total Sales  
- Average Sales  
- Number of Items Sold  
- Average Customer Rating  
- Item Visibility  

---

## Key Insights
- Medium-sized outlets generate the highest sales contribution  
- Tier-3 locations show strong revenue potential  
- Fruits, snacks, and household items are top-performing categories  
- Grocery stores and supermarkets maintain consistent performance  
- Customer ratings remain stable across outlet types
